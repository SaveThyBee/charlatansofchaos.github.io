About us...

We are a quartet of role-playing game (RPG) enthusiasts
who hail from the Northeastern part of the United States.
After starting our first Dungeons & Dragons campaign
last fall, we have been playing together as a group for over
a year now. We invite you to learn more about our adventures
by exploring this website, and if you are interested in joining
in on the action please send us an email on our contact page!
