Meet the Characters

Here are a few of the characters we have created and
played through some of our campagins. Each description
will give you a brief biography on the character.

Andraste
Andraste is a wood-elf ranger. She is a tenth level
character from our Island of the Gods campain.
She is blind, but uses her falcon named Farsight
to see. She comes from an outlander background
with a chaotic good alignment. She is played by
Autumn.
Stats and picture to come.
Isten
Isten is a tiefling bard. He is a fifth level
character from our Oh, Seven Hells! campain.
His favorite instruments to play are the lute and the
bagpipes. He comes from a noble background with a
with a chaotic neutral alignment. He is played by
Jayberwocky. Picture and stats to come.
Silas
Silas is a human gunslinger. He is a fifth level
character from our There Be Pirates campain.
He is a rather broody and sullen character with a
tendency to deliver justice one shot at a time. He
comes from a pirate background with a lawful neutral
alignment. He is played by Jayberwocky.
Picture and stats to come.
